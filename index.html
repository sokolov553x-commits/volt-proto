How it works

Four steps
to full battery

No cables. No waiting. Find the nearest station on the map, scan the QR code, take a powerbank and charge on the go. Return at any location in the network.

01
Find a station

Open the app and see all nearby stations with live availability counts.

02
Scan & unlock

Scan the station QR or tap NFC. A powerbank releases in under 30 seconds.

03
Charge your device

Up to 10,000 mAh. Keep it as long as you need — 1,500 TZS first hour, 7,000 TZS daily cap.

04
Return anywhere

Drop it at any VOLT station in DSM. Payment auto-collected on return.
